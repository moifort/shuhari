# Changelog

All notable changes to Shuhari, most recent first.

## 1.0 (2026.07.21)

### New

- Import a recipe from a photo, a link or plain text: the AI extracts the title, ingredients, steps and course.
- Rate an attempt from 1 to 5 stars, with a remark and photos.
- From a remark, the AI proposes the next version of the recipe, flagging every changed quantity and the reason for it.
- Every version of a recipe stays available; the best-rated one opens by default.
- Notebook: sort by type or by course, favourites, and a list of versions still to cook.
- Thermomix recipes: speed, temperature, duration and reverse kept on every step.
- Cooking tips proposed for each version.
- Export the notebook to a file, restore it, and delete the account from Settings.
- The notebook is free and unlimited; AI calls are metered monthly, and Premium lifts the limits and adds importing from a web link.
